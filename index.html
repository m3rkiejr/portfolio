Accomplished Restaurateur moving towards a career in foodservice-structured software and web development. Looking to merge front & back of house experience with front & back end solutions.

I first learned to program on a Commodore 64, an experience that seeded the knowledge of logic and control flow at a very early age. Without any programming related courses available in high school, I relied on technical books, magazines, and employment with a local ISP to learn more on the subjects. I was able to achieve a better understanding of algorithms and programming after a year of studies at Virginia Tech under the Computer Science Department. I left university to open a full service restaurant with my family, and after 18 successful years in operation, I decided it was time to get back to the BASICs.
